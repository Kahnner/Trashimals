  1. Respect Everyone: Every member deserves to feel safe and valued. Harassment, bullying, discrimination, hate speech, or targeted attacks will not be tolerated. If you experience or witness inappropriate behavior, immediately report it to a moderator for proper handling.

  3. Nicknames & Avatars: Usernames, profile images, and avatars should remain appropriate and free of offensive or disruptive content.
  4. Respect Artists: Give credit when sharing art or creative content. Do not trace, copy, or misuse the work of others.
